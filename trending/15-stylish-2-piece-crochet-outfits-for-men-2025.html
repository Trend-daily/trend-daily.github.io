15 Stylish 2-Piece Crochet Outfits for Men in 2025



By TrendDaily | August 19, 2025



Crochet isn’t just a summer staple—it’s a bold menswear move in 2025. Two-piece crochet sets are dominating for festivals, city hangs, beach trips, and even upscale events. Below are 15 stylish 2-piece crochet outfits every man should try in 2025—each with a simple styling formula to save, shop, and rock with confidence.

From relaxed co-ords to tailored sets, these looks bring texture, swagger, and effortless versatility. Pin your favorites and build a wardrobe that stands out.

1. Short-Sleeve Shirt & Shorts

Formula: Crochet short-sleeve button-up + matching shorts + loafers

Occasion: Beach outings, casual meetups, summer barbecues

Why It Works: Breathable open weave meets laid-back tailoring for warm days.

Pro Tip: Add a bucket hat and sunglasses for instant vacation swagger.



2. Oversized Polo & Joggers

Formula: Crochet polo + drawstring joggers + chunky sneakers

Occasion: Streetwear looks, coffee runs, casual gigs

Why It Works: Relaxed fit with textured knit elevates basic loungewear.

Pro Tip: Go for muted tones like charcoal or olive for easy mixing.



3. Camp Shirt & Trousers

Formula: Crochet camp shirt + relaxed trousers + leather sandals

Occasion: Festivals, summer parties, rooftop drinks

Why It Works: Breezy fit and bold texture channel effortless cool.

Pro Tip: Roll the sleeves and add a woven belt for extra flair.



4. Tank & Wide-Leg Pants

Formula: Crochet tank + wide-leg pants + sleek sneakers

Occasion: Date nights, art events, upscale lounges

Why It Works: Tailored yet relaxed, with knit texture adding depth.

Pro Tip: Layer a lightweight blazer for a polished edge.



5. Long-Sleeve Shirt & Shorts

Formula: Long-sleeve crochet shirt + tailored shorts + loafers

Occasion: Evening strolls, casual dinners, travel days

Why It Works: Balances coverage and breathability with a refined vibe.

Pro Tip: Opt for darker hues like navy or black for evening versatility.



6. Hoodie & Cargo Pants

Formula: Crochet hoodie + cargo pants + high-top sneakers

Occasion: Streetwear days, concerts, urban adventures

Why It Works: Functional pockets meet knit texture for a rugged edge.

Pro Tip: Add a crossbody bag for hands-free street cred.



7. Crewneck & Joggers

Formula: Crochet crewneck + matching joggers + slip-on sneakers

Occasion: Casual hangouts, travel days, coffee runs

Why It Works: Soft texture and relaxed fit redefine cozy style.

Pro Tip: Layer with a denim jacket for extra dimension.



8. Cardigan & Trousers

Formula: Open-front crochet cardigan + slim trousers + loafers

Occasion: Creative offices, dinner parties, art galleries

Why It Works: Lightweight layering adds sophistication without bulk.

Pro Tip: Pair with a fitted tee for a clean, modern silhouette.



9. Vest & Shorts

Formula: Crochet vest + tailored shorts + sandals

Occasion: Beach trips, summer festivals, casual outings

Why It Works: Sleeveless design keeps it cool and stylishly open.

Pro Tip: Add a chain necklace for a subtle, trendy accent.



10. Blazer & Trousers

Formula: Crochet blazer + matching trousers + dress shoes

Occasion: Summer weddings, upscale events, garden parties

Why It Works: Tailored knit brings breathable elegance to formal settings.

Pro Tip: Keep the shirt minimal (white tee or linen) to let the set shine.



11. Fringe Shirt & Shorts

Formula: Crochet fringe shirt + shorts + high-top sneakers

Occasion: Parties, music festivals, night outs

Why It Works: Fringe adds motion and flair for standout moments.

Pro Tip: Keep accessories minimal to let the fringe steal the show.



12. Kimono & Swim Trunks

Formula: Crochet kimono + matching swim trunks + slides

Occasion: Beach clubs, pool parties, resort lounging

Why It Works: Sheer knit layers add style without overheating.

Pro Tip: Choose quick-dry yarns for seamless beach-to-bar transitions.



13. Pullover & Shorts

Formula: Crochet pullover + relaxed shorts + espadrilles

Occasion: City strolls, casual lunches, summer outings

Why It Works: Minimalist design with knit texture feels fresh and versatile.

Pro Tip: Pair with a woven tote for a laid-back summer vibe.



14. Button-Up & Trousers

Formula: Crochet button-up + slim-fit trousers + loafers

Occasion: Dinner dates, creative meetings, upscale casual events

Why It Works: Structured knit offers breathable, tailored style.

Pro Tip: Tuck the shirt and add a slim belt for a clean finish.



15. Tank & Tailored Pants

Formula: Crochet tank + tailored pants + dress sneakers

Occasion: Smart-casual events, date nights, gallery openings

Why It Works: Clean lines and knit texture create a modern, refined look.

Pro Tip: Add a lightweight jacket for cooler evenings or extra polish.



Conclusion

Two-piece crochet sets for men bring texture, versatility, and bold style to 2025. Save your favorite looks, mix and match these co-ords, and you’ll be set for every vibe this year. Tag #TDCrochetMens to show off your fits.
